Sahari Dua

نَوَيْتُ اَنْ اَصُوْمَ غَدًا مِّنْ شَهْرِ رَمْضَانَ الْمُبَارَكِ فَرْضًا لَكَ يَا اللهُ

“I intend to fast tomorrow for the month of Ramadan, obligatory for You, O Allah.”

Iftar Dua

اَللَّهُمَّ لَكَ صُمْتُ وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ

“O Allah, for You I have fasted and with Your provision I break my fast.”

Ram Date Sahari Iftar

Sahari Tips

  • Eat complex carbs (oats, brown rice) for long energy.
  • Drink 2–2.5 litres of water slowly.
  • Include dates & yogurt.

Iftar Tips

  • Start with 3 dates + water (Sunnah).
  • Wait 10–15 minutes before full meal.
  • Add fruits & avoid heavy fried food.
