রমজান তারিখ সেহরি ইফতার

Sahari Essentials

  • 1 Drink water slowly, avoid ice cold water.
  • 2 Eat fiber-rich food like dates, oats, or whole grain.
  • 3 A bit of yogurt helps prevent acidity.

Iftar Essentials

  • 1 Break fast with 3 dates (Sunnah).
  • 2 Avoid deep-fried "Piyaju/Beguni" in large amounts.
  • 3 Give your stomach 10 mins before heavy meal.
