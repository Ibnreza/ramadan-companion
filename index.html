Sahari Tips
Fuel your fast wisely
Eat slowly-digesting foods like oats, eggs, or brown rice to stay full longer.
Drink at least 1 litre of water slowly — avoid gulping all at once.
Limit caffeine (tea/coffee) — it increases fluid loss and causes dehydration.
Include bananas or yogurt for sustained energy and gut health.
